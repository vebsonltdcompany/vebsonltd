About Vebson Ltd

Vebson Ltd is an International Business Company incorporated in Saint Lucia on September 22, 2023, under registration number 2023-00468. As a licensed and regulated entity, we specialize in providing brokerage services for foreign exchange (FX) and contracts for difference (CFDs). Our operations are based in the beautiful island of Saint Lucia, with our registered office at Ground Floor, The Sotheby Building, Rodney Bay, Gros-Islet, Saint Lucia.

Led by our Director and Sole Shareholder, Benchuan Xue, we are committed to offering secure, transparent, and innovative trading solutions to clients worldwide. Our legal standing is affirmed through apostille and notarial certifications, ensuring compliance with international standards.
